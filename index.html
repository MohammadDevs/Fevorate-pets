1. What breed of dogs do you sell?

Answer: We offer a variety of dog breeds to suit different lifestyles and preferences. Whether you're looking for a small companion or an active working dog, we have options like Labradors, French Bulldogs, Poodles, and many more. You can browse our available breeds on our homepage or use the breed filter to find your perfect match!

2. Are the dogs purebred?

Answer: Yes, all of our dogs are purebred, and we provide the necessary documentation to prove their lineage. Our dogs come from reputable breeders who follow ethical breeding practices to ensure healthy and happy puppies.

3. What is the price range for your dogs?

Answer: The price of our dogs varies depending on the breed, age, and pedigree. You can find the price details on each dog’s individual listing. If you have any specific questions about pricing, feel free to contact us, and we’d be happy to assist!
